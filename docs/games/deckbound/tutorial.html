⚠ SUPERSEDED — Clash mechanics. This walkthrough teaches the older stance/Edge duel (Marshal · Unleash · Overwhelm · Parry over a tracked Edge meter). The canonical tactical core is now the Clash: a beat-by-beat duel where each beat both fighters secretly pick one of six moves — Strike · Throw · Parry · Evade (standing) plus Charge · Recover (setups) — durable face-up Charge cards double damage (×2 each), and the duel runs to Body 0 (Body-attrition, not ends-on-first-strike). See spec §1.0 The Clash for the rules and the-duel.md for the design rationale. Kept for design history; a Clash-based walkthrough is pending.
