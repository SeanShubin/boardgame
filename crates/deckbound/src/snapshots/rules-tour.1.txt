GLOSSARY   (what the rulebook says about each keyword above)
  Backstab               Bonus damage when this Outrider strikes an enemy Rearguard.
  Blitz                  The first slip each round is free — it costs no Tempo.
  Bulwark                Holds the line for the whole front — every allied Vanguard's hold rises, not only this Wall's.
  Damage (Might)         Deals 0 untyped Might. It accumulates into the round's pile; each time the pile clears the target's Toughness bar, one Health card turns face down (pile → bar → pool, §2.2); Force scales this.
  Empower                Raises allies' Might by 2 this round (an Empower) — the Support buff's indirect offense.
  Haste                  Grants +3 Tempo to an ally (a Haste).
  Lifeline (Last Stand)  Last Stand: this round the holder cannot be downed — damage that would fell it leaves it at 1 health (M3).
  Mend                   Restores 4 Health cards to the most-wounded ally (a Mend).
  Phalanx                Wall chargers who stop together combine their effort to intercept runners — several defenders hold the front as one.
  Rout                   Drives the target from the line to the Rearguard this round (a Rout) — a Controller status (§4).
  Shadowstep             Win a tie when slipping past an interceptor — your equal Tempo gets you through anyway.
  Stagger                On a landed hit, the target loses its action this round.
  Taunt                  Draws fire: chargers are pulled to intercept this Wall first, sparing the rest of the line.
  Zone: Spend            Zone — Spend: the card goes face-down (Down) after use; only a Recover stands it back up. That gap is its cooldown (§5.3).
